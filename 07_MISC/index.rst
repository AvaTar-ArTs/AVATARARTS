* **Digital Marketing & SEO** - Content optimization, YouTube analytics, SEO tools
* **Service Businesses** - Cleaning services, retention platforms, marketplaces
* **Content Creation** - Music assets, AI-generated content, web galleries
* **Client Services** - Custom websites, SEO projects, professional services
