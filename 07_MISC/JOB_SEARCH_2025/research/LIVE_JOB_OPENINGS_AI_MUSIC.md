### Method 1: Direct Application (Fastest)

For each company above:
1. Click the careers link
2. Search for: "engineer" OR "ML" OR "data"
3. Filter by: Engineering, Machine Learning, Data
4. Apply to 2-3 positions per company

### Method 2: LinkedIn Search

```
https://www.linkedin.com/jobs/search/?keywords=engineer&f_C=[COMPANY_ID]&f_TPR=r604800
```

**Find Company ID:**
1. Go to company LinkedIn page
2. URL shows: `linkedin.com/company/[COMPANY_ID]/`
3. Use that ID in search URL

### Method 3: Set Up Email Alerts

For each company:
1. Go to careers page
2. Click "Join Talent Network" or "Get Alerts"
3. Get notified when new jobs post
