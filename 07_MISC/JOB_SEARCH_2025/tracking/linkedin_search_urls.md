### Option 1: Click Directly

2. Paste in browser
3. Browse results
4. Apply to relevant positions

### Option 2: Save Searches
1. Click URL
2. Click "Save search" on LinkedIn
3. Enable email alerts
4. Get notified of new jobs daily

### Option 3: Boolean Search
LinkedIn supports boolean operators in the search box:
- `AND` - both terms must appear
- `OR` - either term can appear
- `NOT` - exclude term
- `""` - exact phrase

**Example:**
```
"machine learning" AND (music OR audio) NOT senior
```

## ?? Advanced LinkedIn Search Tips

### 1. Use Quotes for Exact Phrases
```
"machine learning engineer" "generative AI"
```

### 2. Use Parentheses for Grouping
```
engineer (music OR audio OR generative)
```

### 3. Filter by Date Posted
- Past 24 hours: `f_TPR=r86400`
- Past week: `f_TPR=r604800`
- Past month: `f_TPR=r2592000`

### 4. Filter by Experience Level
- Entry level: `f_E=2`
- Mid-Senior: `f_E=3,4`
- Director+: `f_E=5,6`

### 5. Filter by Company Size
- 1-10: Startup
- 11-50: Small
- 51-200: Medium
- 201-500: Mid-size
- 501+: Large

### 6. Filter by Remote
- Remote: `f_WT=2`
- On-site: `f_WT=1`
- Hybrid: `f_WT=3`
