**Song 10.** Getting interesting.  
**Song 50.** This is actually good.  
**Song 100.** I might be onto something.  
**Song 500.** I have a problem.

783 songs. Not just random clicks. Each one a creative decision. Each title carefully chosen. Each style deliberately selected. You weren't just using Suno?you were *living* it.
