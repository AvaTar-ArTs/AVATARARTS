Generic Prompt Template for Narrative-Driven Image Generation

Use this template to replicate similar images while adjusting for specific themes, objects, and emotions:

Generic Prompt Template

  • Scene Description: "Create a [type of shot] ([close-up/wide/panoramic]) of [main subject or action] in [specific setting/environment]. The scene should convey [emotional tone or mood]."
  • Key Visual Elements: "Include [specific objects, characters, or details] prominently in the image. Ensure [specific interactions or arrangements] are visually clear."
  • Lighting and Colors: "Use [specific lighting style] (e.g., warm, soft, dramatic) and [color palette] (e.g., vibrant, muted) to emphasize the mood and atmosphere."
  • Typography or Text (Optional): "If text is included, display [specific phrase or lyrics] in [style of typography] (e.g., glowing graffiti, etched into objects) integrated seamlessly into the scene."
  • Style/Art Direction: "Adopt a [specific artistic style] (e.g., retro, futuristic, whimsical) with attention to [specific design elements like texture, reflections, or dynamic lighting]."

Example Adaptations

Here are examples of how this generic prompt can be customized for different scenarios:

1. Nostalgic Vinyl Record

  • Scene Description: "Create a close-up shot of a spinning vinyl record in a cozy vintage room illuminated by soft bulbs. The scene should convey nostalgia and warmth."
  • Key Visual Elements: "Include the vinyl record as the central focus with typography etched into its grooves reading 'Echoes of Truth.' Surround it with subtle vintage details like an old turntable and warm lighting."
  • Lighting and Colors: "Use soft amber and deep brown tones to enhance the nostalgic atmosphere."
  • Typography or Text: "Display the phrase 'Echoes of Truth' etched into the grooves of the record."
  • Style/Art Direction: "Adopt a retro aesthetic with attention to texture and light reflections on the vinyl surface."

2. Rebellious Campfire

  • Scene Description: "Create a wide shot of TrashCats gathered around a glowing campfire in an open field under the night sky. The scene should convey rebellion and camaraderie."
  • Key Visual Elements: "Include sparks rising from the fire that form glowing lyrics in mid-air reading 'Stories Old, Hearts Bold.' Surround them with scattered objects like guitars or cans to add detail."
  • Lighting and Colors: "Use vibrant orange and red tones for firelight, contrasting with muted blues for the night sky."
  • Typography or Text: "Display 'Stories Old, Hearts Bold' as glowing sparks above the fire."
  • Style/Art Direction: "Adopt a whimsical yet rebellious tone with dynamic lighting."

3. Urban Sunset Rebellion

  • Scene Description: "Create a zoomed-out view of an urban skyline at sunset. The scene should convey rebellion blended with hope."
  • Key Visual Elements: "Include vibrant graffiti on building walls reading 'Chaos is Life,' prominently displayed as part of the urban environment."
  • Lighting and Colors: "Use rich sunset hues like orange, pink, and purple to contrast against bold graffiti colors like red and black."
  • Typography or Text: "Display 'Chaos is Life' as bold graffiti integrated into building walls."
  • Style/Art Direction: "Adopt a gritty yet hopeful tone blending urban rebellion with natural beauty."

4. Whispered Tales

  • Scene Description: "Create a detailed nighttime scene of raccoons gathered under dim streetlights whispering stories. The scene should convey intimacy and mystery."
  • Key Visual Elements: "Include glowing graffiti on nearby walls reading 'Battles Won.' Surround them with subtle details like scattered trash cans or shadows cast by streetlights."
  • Lighting and Colors: "Use muted tones like gray and blue for the background and soft yellow for streetlights to enhance the mysterious atmosphere."
  • Typography or Text: "Display 'Battles Won' as softly glowing graffiti integrated into nearby walls."
  • Style/Art Direction: "Adopt an intimate yet mysterious tone with attention to shadow play."
