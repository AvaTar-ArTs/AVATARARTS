### What Is SORA?
SORA is a high-quality text-to-video AI model designed to transform written scripts or storyboards into cinematic video content. - The ultimate goal is to tell *your* story, not just to produce “a” video.

3. **Recut**  
   - Analyze the initial output.  
   - If the color palette is off or the pacing is too slow/fast, revise the text.

4. **Visual Continuity**  
   - Maintain a consistent color scheme (neon blues, pinks, and purples for night; a warm golden glow for dawn).

5. **Audio/Voiceover**  
   - You can feed SORA text-based instructions for background music style (“punk rock vibes” or “low-fi heartbreak guitar”).
