### **Generic Prompt Template for Narrative-Driven Image Generation**

Use this template to replicate similar images while adjusting for specific themes, objects, and emotions:

---

#### **Generic Prompt Template**

- **Scene Description:**
"Create a [type of shot] ([close-up/wide/panoramic]) of [main subject or action] in [specific setting/environment]. The scene should convey [emotional tone or mood]."
- **Key Visual Elements:**
"Include [specific objects, characters, or details] prominently in the image. Ensure [specific interactions or arrangements] are visually clear."
- **Lighting and Colors:**
"Use [specific lighting style] (e.g., warm, soft, dramatic) and [color palette] (e.g., vibrant, muted) to emphasize the mood and atmosphere."
- **Typography or Text (Optional):**
"If text is included, display [specific phrase or lyrics] in [style of typography] (e.g., glowing graffiti, etched into objects) integrated seamlessly into the scene."
- **Style/Art Direction:**
"Adopt a [specific artistic style] (e.g., retro, futuristic, whimsical) with attention to [specific design elements like texture, reflections, or dynamic lighting]."
