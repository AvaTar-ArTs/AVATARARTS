What is TMS therapy like?

TMS therapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain. The procedure is painless, takes about 20-30 minutes, and you can return to normal activities immediately after. Most patients feel a gentle tapping sensation on their scalp during treatment.
