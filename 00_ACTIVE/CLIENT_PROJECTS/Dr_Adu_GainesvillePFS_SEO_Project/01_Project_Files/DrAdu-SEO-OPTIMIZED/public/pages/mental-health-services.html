Mental Health Services

Conditions We Treat

Dr. Lawrence Adu provides comprehensive mental health services for a wide range of psychiatric conditions. Our practice offers evidence-based treatments tailored to each patient's unique needs.

Depression

Comprehensive treatment for major depressive disorder, including medication management and innovative TMS therapy for treatment-resistant depression.

  • Major depressive disorder
  • Treatment-resistant depression
  • Persistent depressive disorder
  • Seasonal affective disorder

Anxiety Disorders

Evidence-based treatment for various anxiety disorders, helping patients manage symptoms and improve quality of life.

  • Generalized anxiety disorder
  • Panic disorder
  • Social anxiety disorder
  • Obsessive-compulsive disorder

Bipolar Disorder

Comprehensive treatment for bipolar disorder, including mood stabilization and ongoing management to prevent episodes.

  • Bipolar I disorder
  • Bipolar II disorder
  • Cyclothymic disorder
  • Mood stabilization

Addiction & Substance Use

Specialized treatment for substance use disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions, using evidence-based approaches.

  • Substance use disorders
  • Alcohol use disorder
  • Opioid use disorder
  • Dual diagnosis treatment

Other Conditions

Treatment for various other mental health conditions, including PTSD, ADHD, and other psychiatric disorders.

  • Post-traumatic stress disorder (PTSD)
  • Attention-deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD)
  • Personality disorders
  • Other psychiatric conditions

Treatment Approaches

Medication Management

Careful evaluation and management of psychiatric medications, with regular monitoring to ensure optimal effectiveness and minimal side effects.

TMS Therapy

Innovative Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation therapy for treatment-resistant depression, offering a non-invasive alternative to traditional treatments.

Comprehensive Evaluation

Thorough psychiatric evaluations to accurately diagnose conditions and develop personalized treatment plans.

Ongoing Care

Regular follow-up appointments to monitor progress, adjust treatment, and provide ongoing support for your mental health journey.

Our Approach to Mental Health Care

At Gainesville Psychiatry and Forensic Services, we believe in providing compassionate, patient-centered care. Dr. Adu takes the time to understand each patient's unique situation and develop comprehensive treatment plans that address their specific needs.

Key Principles:

  • Evidence-Based Treatment: Using treatments supported by scientific research
  • Personalized Care: Tailoring treatment to each patient's unique needs
  • Comprehensive Approach: Addressing all aspects of mental health
  • Collaborative Care: Working with patients and their families
  • Ongoing Support: Providing continuous care and monitoring
