Professional Background

Dr. Lawrence Adu is a board-certified psychiatrist with over 17 years of experience providing exceptional mental health care to patients in Gainesville, Florida, and surrounding areas. He is the founder and medical director of Gainesville Psychiatry and Forensic Services, a comprehensive psychiatric practice offering a wide range of specialized services.

Education & Training

Dr. Adu completed his medical education and psychiatric residency training at accredited institutions, earning his board certification in psychiatry. His commitment to continuing education ensures that he stays current with the latest advances in psychiatric treatment and evidence-based practices.

Areas of Expertise

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS)

Dr. Adu is a certified provider of NeuroStar TMS therapy, offering FDA-approved, non-invasive treatment for treatment-resistant depression. He has extensive experience helping patients who haven't responded to traditional antidepressant medications.

Forensic Psychiatry

With specialized training in forensic psychiatry, Dr. Adu provides expert evaluations for legal proceedings, competency assessments, and expert witness services. His forensic expertise is recognized throughout the Florida legal system.

Addiction Medicine

Dr. Adu offers comprehensive addiction medicine services, providing evidence-based treatment for substance use disorders and co-occurring mental health conditions.

General Psychiatry

Dr. Adu provides comprehensive psychiatric evaluation and treatment for a wide range of mental health conditions, including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, and other mood and anxiety disorders.

Philosophy of Care

Dr. Adu believes in providing personalized, compassionate care that addresses each patient's unique needs. He takes the time to listen to his patients, understand their concerns, and develop comprehensive treatment plans that may include medication management, therapy, and innovative treatments like TMS therapy.

"My goal is to help each patient achieve their best possible mental health and quality of life. I believe in using the most effective, evidence-based treatments while maintaining a compassionate, patient-centered approach to care."
