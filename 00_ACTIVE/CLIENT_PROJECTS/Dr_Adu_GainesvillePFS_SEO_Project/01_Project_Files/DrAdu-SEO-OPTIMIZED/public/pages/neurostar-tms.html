What is TMS Therapy Like? Complete Guide to TMS Treatment Gainesville FL

TMS therapy Gainesville FL is a revolutionary, non-invasive treatment for depression that uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain. Unlike traditional treatments, TMS therapy doesn't require medication or anesthesia, making it a safe and effective option for patients who haven't responded to antidepressants.

How TMS Therapy Works

During TMS treatment, a magnetic coil is placed against your scalp near your forehead. The coil delivers brief magnetic pulses that stimulate nerve cells in the brain regions involved in mood control. This stimulation helps restore normal brain function and alleviate depression symptoms.

TMS Therapy Process

  • Initial Consultation: Dr. Adu evaluates your condition and determines if TMS is right for you
  • Treatment Planning: Customized treatment plan based on your specific needs
  • Daily Sessions: 20-30 minute sessions, typically 5 days per week for 4-6 weeks
  • Progress Monitoring: Regular assessments to track improvement and adjust treatment

TMS Therapy Side Effects - What to Expect

Common Side Effects (Mild and Temporary)

  • Scalp Discomfort: Mild discomfort at the treatment site during sessions
  • Headache: Occasional mild headache that typically resolves quickly
  • Facial Twitching: Brief muscle twitching during treatment (normal response)
  • Lightheadedness: Rare, mild lightheadedness that resolves quickly

Serious Side Effects (Very Rare)

Serious side effects are extremely rare with TMS therapy. The most serious potential side effect is seizure, which occurs in less than 0.1% of patients. Dr. Adu carefully screens all patients to minimize this risk.
