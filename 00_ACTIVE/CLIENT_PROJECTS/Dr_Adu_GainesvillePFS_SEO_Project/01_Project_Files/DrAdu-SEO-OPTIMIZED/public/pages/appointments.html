What to Expect

Initial Consultation

Your first appointment will include a comprehensive psychiatric evaluation. Dr. Adu will review your medical history, discuss your concerns, and develop a personalized treatment plan.

Follow-Up Appointments

Regular follow-up appointments allow Dr. Adu to monitor your progress, adjust treatment as needed, and provide ongoing support for your mental health journey.

Treatment Options

Based on your evaluation, Dr. Adu will discuss appropriate treatment options, which may include medication management, TMS therapy, or other evidence-based treatments.

Before Your Appointment

Please Bring:

  • Valid photo ID
  • Insurance card
  • List of current medications
  • Previous medical records (if applicable)
  • List of questions or concerns

Insurance Information

We accept most major insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card to your appointment, and our staff will verify your benefits and coverage.

New Patient Forms

If you're a new patient, please arrive 15 minutes early to complete necessary paperwork. You may also request forms in advance by calling our office.
