Services We Offer

Dr. Lawrence Adu and Gainesville Psychiatry and Forensic Services provide a comprehensive range of psychiatric services to meet your mental health needs. Our practice combines evidence-based treatments with compassionate, personalized care.

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) Therapy

FDA-approved, non-invasive treatment for treatment-resistant depression. TMS therapy uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain, offering hope for patients who haven't responded to traditional antidepressant medications.

  • NeuroStar TMS technology
  • Non-invasive, no anesthesia required
  • Minimal side effects
  • Insurance coverage available
