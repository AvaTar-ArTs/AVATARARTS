NeuroStar TMS Therapy

Advanced treatment for depression that doesn't respond to medication

Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation (TMS) is a non-invasive procedure that uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain. Our practice uses the latest NeuroStar technology to provide safe, effective treatment for depression.
