Our Foundational Services - Expert Mental Health Care in Gainesville FL

General Psychiatry

Our general psychiatry practice provides comprehensive mental health care for individuals of all ages. We specialize in diagnosing, treating, and managing a wide range of psychiatric conditions, including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, PTSD, and more. Our approach combines personalized medication management with therapeutic support tailored to your unique needs. Whether you are seeking an initial evaluation or ongoing treatment, we are committed to partnering with you on your journey to improved mental wellness and overall quality of life.

Forensic Psychiatry

Our forensic psychiatry practice provides specialized evaluations and expert consultation at the intersection of mental health and the legal system. We offer comprehensive assessments related to criminal and civil cases, including competency evaluations, risk assessments, criminal responsibility, and fitness for trial. Our team delivers objective, evidence-based opinions and expert testimony to assist courts, attorneys, and other legal professionals in making informed decisions. We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of professionalism, confidentiality, and clinical expertise throughout every case.

Addiction Medicine

Our addiction medication management services provide personalized, evidence-based treatment to support recovery from substance use disorders. We utilize FDA-approved medications to help manage cravings, reduce withdrawal symptoms, and support long-term sobriety. Combined with counseling and behavioral therapies, medication-assisted treatment (MAT) offers a comprehensive approach to addiction recovery. Our goal is to provide compassionate care that addresses both the physical and psychological aspects of addiction, helping you regain control and improve your quality of life.

About Our Practice - Leading Mental Health Services in Gainesville FL

Located in the heart of Gainesville, Florida, our psychiatry practice is dedicated to providing compassionate, evidence-based mental health care to individuals and families. Led by a board-certified psychiatrist, we offer personalized treatment plans tailored to your unique needs, including medication management, psychotherapy, and specialty services. Our mission is to help you achieve lasting wellness in a supportive and respectful environment.
