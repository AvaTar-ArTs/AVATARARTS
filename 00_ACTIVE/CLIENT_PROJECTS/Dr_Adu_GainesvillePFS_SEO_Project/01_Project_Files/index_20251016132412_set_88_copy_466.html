Frequently Asked Questions

What is TMS therapy and how does it work?

TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) therapy is FDA-approved, non-invasive, and used for depression. It uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in brain areas associated with mood regulation.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Medicaid, and more. Call to verify your coverage.

How long does a typical appointment last?

Initial consultations last about 60-90 minutes; follow-ups are 30-45 minutes. TMS sessions last 20-40 minutes.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, PTSD, ADHD, OCD, addiction, and more.
