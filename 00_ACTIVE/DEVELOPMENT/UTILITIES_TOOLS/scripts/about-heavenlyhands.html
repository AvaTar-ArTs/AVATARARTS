Our Story

Heavenly Hands Cleaning Service was founded in 2016 by Steven Rodriguez with a simple mission: to provide exceptional cleaning services that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest standards of professionalism and reliability.

What started as a small, family-owned business has grown into Gainesville & Ocala's most trusted cleaning service. Kimberly's commitment to quality, attention to detail, and genuine care for her customers has earned Heavenly Hands a reputation for excellence throughout Central Florida.

Today, we serve hundreds of satisfied customers across Gainesville, Ocala, and surrounding areas. Our team of trained professionals continues to uphold the same values that Kimberly established when she first opened the doors to Heavenly Hands.

Meet Steven Rodriguez

Owner & Operator

Steven Rodriguez is the heart and soul of Heavenly Hands Cleaning Service. With over 8 years of experience in the cleaning industry, Steven brings a unique combination of business acumen, hands-on expertise, and genuine care for his customers.

Kimberly's journey in the cleaning industry began when she recognized the need for reliable, professional cleaning services in Gainesville & Ocala. Her attention to detail and commitment to customer satisfaction quickly earned her a loyal following of residential and commercial clients.

As the owner, Steven personally oversees every aspect of the business, from customer service to quality control. He believes that a clean space is essential for health, productivity, and peace of mind, and he's dedicated to providing that for every customer.
