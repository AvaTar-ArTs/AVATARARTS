## YouTube SEO

### YouTube Algorithm Factors

**Ranked by Importance:**
1. Click-Through Rate (CTR) - Thumbnail + Title
2. Average View Duration / Watch Time
3. Session Time (keeps viewers on YouTube)
4. Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
5. Upload frequency and consistency
6. Channel authority

### Video Title Optimization

**Formula:** [Hook] + [Keyword] + [Benefit/Number]

**Best Practices:**
- Length: 60-70 characters (mobile display)
- Front-load important keywords
- Include numbers when relevant
- Create curiosity but don't clickbait
- Use power words

**Title Templates:**

```
How to [Achieve Result] - [Method] in [Timeframe]
Example: How to Rank #1 on Google - 5 Simple Steps in 30 Days

[Number] [Adjective] Ways to [Achieve Result]
Example: 7 Proven Ways to Increase YouTube Views by 300%

[Result] Without [Common Obstacle]
Example: Grow Your Channel Without Spending Money on Ads

The [Superlative] [Topic] Guide for [Year]
Example: The Complete YouTube SEO Guide for 2025

[Action] Like [Authority Figure/Brand]
Example: Edit Videos Like MrBeast (Budget-Friendly)

I [Action] for [Time Period] - Here's What Happened
Example: I Posted Daily on YouTube for 90 Days - Here's What Happened
```

**Good vs Bad Examples:**

```

### Thumbnail Design

**Specifications:**
- Size: 1280 x 720 pixels (16:9 ratio)
- Format: JPG, PNG, GIF, BMP
- Max file size: 2MB
- Resolution: 1920 x 1080 for HD

**Design Best Practices:**

**Text Elements:**
- Max 3-5 words
- Readable on mobile (test at 360px wide)
- Contrasting colors
- Large, bold fonts
- Avoid YouTube's red play button area (bottom right)

**Visual Elements:**
- Close-up of face showing emotion
- Bright, contrasting colors
- Avoid clutter
- Brand consistency
- Arrow/highlight to draw eye

**Thumbnail Formula:**
```
Face with Expression + Text + Contrasting Background + Brand Element
```

**Color Combinations That Work:**
- Yellow + Black
- Red + White
- Blue + Orange
- Green + White
- Purple + Yellow

### Video File Optimization

**Before Upload:**
```
File name: target-keyword-description.mp4
Example: youtube-seo-tutorial-2025.mp4
```

**Why it matters:** YouTube can read your filename before processing.

### End Screens & Cards

**End Screens (Last 5-20 seconds):**
- Link to next video (related content)
- Playlist link
- Subscribe button
- Channel link

**Cards (During video):**
- Link to related video at relevant moment
- Poll cards for engagement
- Link to playlist
- Max 5 cards per video

**Best Practices:**
- Mention cards verbally ("Check the card appearing now")
- Place cards at natural breaks
- Link to high-performing content

### Playlists Strategy

**Benefits:**
- Increases watch time
- Better organization
- Appears in search results
- Auto-plays next video

**Optimization:**
- Keyword-rich playlist titles
- Detailed descriptions
- Strategic video order
- Custom thumbnail for playlist

### YouTube Shorts (under 60 seconds)

**Optimization:**
- Vertical format (9:16)
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Add #Shorts in title or description
- Use trending sounds
- Include captions
- End with strong CTA

**Best performing formats:**
- Quick tips
- Before/After
- Common mistakes
- Myth busting
- Quick tutorials

## TikTok Optimization

### TikTok Algorithm Factors

**Ranked by Importance:**
1. Watch time (especially completion rate)
2. Re-watches
3. Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
4. Video information (sounds, hashtags, captions)
5. Account settings (language, location)

### TikTok Video Optimization

**Video Specs:**
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical)
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 pixels minimum
- Length: 15-60 seconds optimal (up to 10 minutes available)
- Format: MP4 or MOV
- Max file size: 287.6 MB (iOS), 72 MB (Android)

**Hook Formula (First 3 Seconds):**

```
Pattern Interrupt + Curiosity Gap

‚Üí "This changed everything..."

```

**Caption Best Practices:**
- Max length: 2,200 characters (but shorter is better)
- First line is most important (visible before "see more")
- Include keywords naturally
- Add CTA
- Use spacing for readability
- Include relevant emojis

**Caption Formula:**

```

‚Üì

‚Üì

### TikTok Hashtag Strategy

**Structure:**
- 3-5 hashtags total
- Mix of sizes:
  - 1-2 large (10M+ views)
  - 2-3 medium (100K-1M views)
  - 1-2 niche (10K-100K views)

**Examples:**

## Instagram Reels Strategy

### Instagram Reels Algorithm

**Ranking Factors:**
1. Engagement (saves, shares > likes)
2. Watch time and completion rate
3. Relationship (accounts you interact with)
4. Relevancy (interests and past behavior)
5. Timeliness

### Reels Optimization

**Video Specs:**
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 (vertical)
- Resolution: 1080 x 1920 pixels
- Length: 15-90 seconds (60 seconds ideal)
- Format: MP4
- Max file size: 4GB

- Readable text
- Brand consistent
- Shows what reel is about

### Instagram Hashtag Strategy

**Best Practices:**
- 10-20 hashtags (hide below dots in caption)
- Mix of sizes (same as TikTok)
- Create branded hashtag
- Research competitors' hashtags

**Placement Options:**
- In caption (hidden with dots)
- In first comment
- In bio for branded hashtag

### Instagram Reels vs TikTok

| Feature | Instagram Reels | TikTok |
|---------|----------------|---------|
| Max Length | 90 seconds | 10 minutes |
| Optimal Length | 30-60 seconds | 15-30 seconds |
| Discovery | Reels tab + Explore | For You Page |
| Audience | 25-44 years old | 18-34 years old |
| Best for | Polished content | Raw, authentic |
| Monetization | Reels Bonus (varies) | Creator Fund |

### Cross-Posting TikTok to Reels

- Download without watermark

## Short-Form Video Best Practices

### Universal Short-Form Rules

**First 3 Seconds:**
- Visual hook
- Text hook
- Audio hook
- Combination of above

**Script Structure:**

```
0-3 sec: Hook (stop the scroll)
3-10 sec: Promise (what they'll learn)
10-40 sec: Deliver value
40-60 sec: CTA + loop back to hook
```

**Example Script:**

```
[0-3] "This SEO hack doubled my traffic"
[3-10] "and it takes 5 minutes to implement"
[10-40] "Here's exactly what to do: [steps]"
[40-60] "Try this now and let me know your results! 
        For more SEO tips, follow @username"
```

### Captions/Subtitles

**Why Use:**
- 85% watch without sound
- Better accessibility
- Higher completion rates
- Better engagement

**Tools:**
- CapCut (free, auto-captions)
- Kapwing (online editor)
- Submagic (paid, animated)
- Rev (professional, paid)

**Styling Tips:**
- Yellow or white text
- Black stroke/background
- Large font size
- Animated when possible

### Hook Formulas That Work

**Question Hooks:**
- "Want to know how to..."
- "What if I told you..."
- "Ever wondered why..."

**Challenge Hooks:**
- "I bet you can't..."
- "Try to [do X] without..."
- "Can you spot..."

**Curiosity Hooks:**
- "The secret to..."
- "Nobody talks about..."
- "What they don't tell you..."

**Authority Hooks:**
- "After [X years/dollars] I learned..."
- "I studied [number] of..."
- "[Number]% of people don't know..."

**FOMO Hooks:**
- "Stop doing [X] and start..."
- "If you're not doing [X]..."
- "The difference between..."

### Common Mistakes to Avoid

**‚ùå Don't:**
- Use lengthy intros
- Bury the value
- Over-edit (appears inauthentic)
- Use low-quality footage
- Ignore trends completely
- Post inconsistently
- Ignore comments

**‚úÖ Do:**
- Hook immediately
- Deliver value quickly
- Stay authentic
- Use good lighting
- Balance trends with original content
- Post daily if possible
- Engage with every comment

## Cross-Platform Distribution

### Repurposing Strategy

**From One Long-Form YouTube Video, Create:**

1. **Full video** (YouTube main channel)
2. **3-5 YouTube Shorts** (key moments)
3. **5-10 TikToks** (separate tips)
4. **5-10 Instagram Reels** (same content as TikTok)
5. **Blog post** (transcript + screenshots)
6. **Twitter thread** (key takeaways)
7. **LinkedIn post** (professional angle)
8. **Email newsletter** (summary + link)
9. **Podcast episode** (audio)
10. **Infographic** (visual summary)

### Platform-Specific Optimization

**YouTube:**
- Length: 8-15 minutes
- Pace: Moderate
- Style: Polished
- CTA: Strong, direct

**TikTok:**
- Length: 15-30 seconds
- Pace: Fast
- Style: Raw, authentic
- CTA: Subtle

**Instagram Reels:**
- Length: 30-60 seconds
- Pace: Medium-fast
- Style: Polished but authentic
- CTA: Conversational

### Posting Schedule

**Optimal Times (Eastern Time):**

**YouTube:**
- Mon-Fri: 2-4 PM
- Weekends: 9-11 AM

**TikTok:**
- Tue-Thu: 6-10 AM, 7-11 PM
- Fri-Mon: 4-6 AM

**Instagram:**
- Mon-Fri: 11 AM, 7-9 PM
- Weekends: 11 AM-1 PM

**Note:** Test these and find what works for YOUR audience.

## Analytics & Performance

### YouTube Analytics

**Key Metrics:**
- **Views:** Total reach
- **Watch Time:** Total minutes watched
- **Average View Duration:** Engagement quality
- **CTR:** Thumbnail + title effectiveness (4-10% good)
- **Retention Rate:** How long viewers stay (50%+ at 30 sec = good)
- **Traffic Sources:** Where viewers come from
- **Subscriber Growth:** Net new subscribers

**Where to Find:**
YouTube Studio ‚Üí Analytics ‚Üí Advanced Mode

### TikTok Analytics

**Key Metrics:**
- **Views:** Total reach
- **Average Watch Time:** Engagement
- **Watch Full Video:** Completion rate
- **Traffic Source:** FYP vs profile vs hashtag
- **Follower Activity:** When followers are online

**Where to Find:**
Profile ‚Üí ‚ò∞ Menu ‚Üí Creator Tools ‚Üí Analytics

**Requirements:** Pro account + 100 followers

### Instagram Reels Analytics

**Key Metrics:**
- **Plays:** Total views
- **Accounts Reached:** Unique viewers
- **Likes, Comments, Saves, Shares:** Engagement
- **Plays from Non-Followers:** Discovery

**Where to Find:**
View Reel ‚Üí View Insights

**Requirements:** Business or Creator account

### Success Benchmarks

**YouTube (Long-form):**
- CTR: 4-10%
- Avg View Duration: 50% of video
- Like Rate: 4-6%
- Comment Rate: 0.5-1%

**YouTube Shorts:**
- Views: 1,000+ in first 24 hours
- Watch Time: 80%+ completion
- Likes: 10% of views

**TikTok:**
- Views: 300+ in first hour
- Completion Rate: 70%+
- Engagement Rate: 10%+

**Instagram Reels:**
- Reach: 20%+ of followers
- Plays: 2x follower count
- Saves: 3%+ of plays

### A/B Testing Ideas

**Test Variables:**
- Thumbnail designs
- Title formulas
- Video lengths
- Hook styles

**Testing Process:**
1. Change one variable
