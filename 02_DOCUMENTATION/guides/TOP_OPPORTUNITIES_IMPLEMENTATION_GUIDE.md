## üö® Risk Mitigation

### Common Risks & Solutions

**Risk**: Low adoption/interest
- **Solution**: Start with free/pilot programs, gather feedback, iterate

**Risk**: Technical issues
- **Solution**: Beta testing, thorough QA, support system

**Risk**: Market competition
- **Solution**: Focus on unique features, first-mover advantage, build moats

**Risk**: Resource constraints
- **Solution**: Prioritize high-ROI opportunities, automate where possible, outsource non-critical tasks
