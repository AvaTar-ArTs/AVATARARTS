### 2. Relevant Experience

**For Video Jobs:**

**For Image Jobs:**

### 3. Portfolio Examples
Reference your relevant work:

- **Music Videos:** "My 'Trashcats' project on Spotify features 100+ AI-generated songs with accompanying visuals"

- **AI Content:** "My content-awareness intelligence system organizes and optimizes AI-generated content at scale"

### 4. Process & Workflow
Outline your systematic approach:

1. **Consultation:** Understand client requirements and vision
2. **Planning:** Create detailed production plan with timelines
3. **Execution:** Use AI tools and automation for efficient production
4. **Review:** Iterate based on feedback
5. **Delivery:** Provide final assets with optimization

### 5. Value Proposition
Explain what sets you apart:

- **Efficiency:** Automation workflows enable faster delivery
- **Quality:** Content-awareness intelligence ensures high standards
- **Experience:** Proven track record with similar projects
- **Scalability:** Systems built for high-volume, consistent delivery

## Pricing Strategy

### For Fixed-Price Jobs
- Research similar completed projects on Upwork
- Factor in your expertise and efficiency
- Consider offering a slight discount for first projects to build reputation
- Example: For a $100 job, bid $80-$100 depending on complexity

### For Hourly Jobs
- Start with mid-range of the offered range
- Example: For $15-$30 range, bid $20-$25
- Justify with your expertise and automation efficiency

## Follow-Up Strategy

1. **Immediate Response:** Respond within 2-4 hours of job posting
2. **Clarification Questions:** Ask thoughtful questions about requirements
3. **Sample Work:** Offer to create a small sample if appropriate
4. **Availability:** Mention your immediate availability for urgent projects

## Red Flags to Avoid

1. **Overpromising:** Don't promise unrealistic timelines
2. **Generic Proposals:** Tailor each proposal to the specific job
3. **Ignoring Requirements:** Address all job requirements in your proposal
4. **Lowballing:** Don't underprice to the point of appearing inexperienced
