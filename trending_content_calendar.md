### **Title Formulas**
- **Tutorial**: "[Action] in [Time] (No [Obstacle] Required)"

- **Case Study**: "How [Person/Company] [Achievement] Using [Method]"
- **Comparison**: "[Tool A] vs [Tool B]: Which is Better for [Use Case]?"

### **Content Structure**
- **Hook**: Problem + solution promise
- **Introduction**: What you'll learn + why it matters
- **Main Content**: Step-by-step instructions
- **Examples**: Real-world applications
- **Conclusion**: Key takeaways + next steps
- **CTA**: Clear action to take

### **SEO Optimization**
- **Primary Keyword**: In title, H1, first paragraph
- **Secondary Keywords**: In H2s and throughout content
- **Long-tail Keywords**: In subheadings and body text
- **Internal Links**: Link to related content and products
- **External Links**: Link to authoritative sources
