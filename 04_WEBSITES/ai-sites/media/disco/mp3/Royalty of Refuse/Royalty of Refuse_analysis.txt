1. **Central Themes and Meaning**:
The song portrays a vivid and unconventional love story set in a gritty, urban environment. It celebrates finding beauty and connection in the midst of decay and neglect. The lyrics emphasize the idea that love can be found in unexpected places, even among discarded items and in neglected spaces. The theme of embracing imperfection and seeing value in what others may overlook is central to the narrative.

2. **Emotional Tone**:
The emotional tone of the song is a mix of defiance, defiance, and a sense of liberation. There's a raw, rebellious energy in the lyrics that exude a feeling of freedom from societal norms and expectations. Despite the harsh surroundings described, there's an underlying sense of joy and camaraderie in the unconventional love story being depicted.

3. **Intent of the Artist**:
The artist seems to be aiming to challenge conventional notions of love and beauty. By setting the love story in a rundown, urban setting filled with trash and decay, the artist is highlighting the resilience of love and the potential for connection to flourish even in the most unlikely circumstances. The intent appears to be to celebrate the unconventional, the overlooked, and the discarded aspects of life.

4. **Significant Metaphors, Symbols, and Imagery**:
- "Broken dreams in shattered glass" symbolizes the harsh realities of life in the urban setting.
- "Rusty hearts beating in sync, wrapped in plastic wrap" evokes the idea of finding love amidst imperfection and decay.
- "Her eyes are diamonds among the broken bottles" juxtaposes beauty with destruction, highlighting the beauty found in unexpected places.
- "Kings and queens of litter in the city's blight" elevates the characters to royalty in their own right, despite their surroundings.

5. **Overall Emotional and Narrative Experience**:
The song creates a unique and evocative narrative experience that challenges traditional romantic narratives. It invites listeners to consider the beauty and resilience that can be found in unconventional love stories and in overlooked spaces. The repeated refrain of "love is rubbish, let's get trashy in the moonlight" reinforces the idea of embracing imperfection and finding value in what others may discard. The instrumental breaks in between the verses help build the atmosphere of the urban landscape and add to the overall emotional depth of the song.
