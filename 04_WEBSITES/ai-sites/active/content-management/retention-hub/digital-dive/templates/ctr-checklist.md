# Thumbnail CTR Checklist (Shorts)

- Triangle of attention: eyes → headline → focal object
- High-visibility zones: top-left, center-right (heatmap aware)
- Emotion: surprise, fear, awe, curiosity (choose one primary)
- Contrast: 3-color max, strong foreground/background separation
- Font: thick, legible at 5% scale; 2-word punchline
- Face: expressive, large; or iconic symbol close-up
- Cleanup: remove clutter, keep 1 primary object + 1 secondary prop
- Test: Option A vs Option B (A/B title + crop)
