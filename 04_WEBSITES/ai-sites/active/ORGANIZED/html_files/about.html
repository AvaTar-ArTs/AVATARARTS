About Quantum Forge Labs

Quantum Forge Labs is an open‐source initiative dedicated to building cutting‐edge tools, libraries, and tutorials for the quantum computing community. Our mission is simple:

  • Democratize quantum research — by providing free, well‐documented, and high‐performance libraries in Python.
  • Accelerate innovation — through tutorials, example notebooks, and reference implementations that help newcomers and experts alike.
  • Community‐driven development — we welcome contributions, bug reports, and collaboration from researchers, students, and hobbyists around the world.

Our Story

Founded in late 2023 by a small team of quantum enthusiasts, Quantum Forge Labs began as a set of personal utilities to simulate small quantum circuits. Over time, we released our first public library—QuantumSim—on PyPI, and soon realized there was a wider appetite for robust, easy‐to‐use quantum tools. Today, we host multiple interlinked projects, including:

  • QuantumSim — GPU‐accelerated circuit simulator
  • QC-API — RESTful interface to quantum backends
  • ErrorCorrLib — Quantum error‐correction code library
