## SEO-Optimized Content Scripts

### Dark Narrative Image Prompts Script
**Purpose**: Generate SEO-optimized narrative-driven image prompts for themes within a "dark narrative" context

#### Key Components:
1. **Theme Configuration**:
   - Fonts: "bold shattered sans-serif" for societal breakdown
   - Color Schemes: "Foreboding" and "surveillance" themes
   - Lighting: "Dramatic side lighting" and "neon rim lighting"

2. **Title Generation**:
   - Function: `generate_theme_title()`
   - Concepts: "Rebellion" and "Echoes"
   - Phrases: "Neon Noir Chronicles"

3. **Image Prompt Construction**:
   - Function: `construct_image_prompt()`
   - Integration: Theme elements and narrative symbols
   - Examples: "surveillance drones", "broken infrastructure"

4. **Batch Generation**:
   - Four unique prompts per batch
   - Continuation concepts for narrative expansion
   - Error handling and data integrity

#### Narrative Themes:
- **Return of Nature**: Nature reclaiming dystopian cityscapes
- **Resistance Movement**: Clandestine gatherings and planning
- **Digital Overload**: Data streams blurring virtual and real
- **Forgotten Ruins**: Ancient structures with modern technology
