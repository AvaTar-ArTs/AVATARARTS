## üìä **Google Analytics**
**What it is:** A free tool from Google that tracks who visits your website.

**What it does:**
- Counts how many people visit your site each day/week/month
- Shows you which pages people look at most
- Tells you if visitors are on phones, tablets, or computers
- Shows you what cities your visitors are from
- Tracks how many people call or email you from your website

**Real example:** "Last month, 150 people visited your website. 80% were on their phones, and 12 people filled out your contact form."

**Why you need it:** You can't improve what you don't measure. This helps you understand if your website is working.

**Cost:** Free

**Why you need it:** Helps Google understand and rank your website.
