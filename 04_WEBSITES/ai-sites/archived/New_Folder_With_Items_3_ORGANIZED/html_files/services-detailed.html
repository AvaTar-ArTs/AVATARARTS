Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the pricing?

All pricing includes source files, commercial usage rights, and the specified number of revisions. Additional services like rush delivery or extra revisions may incur additional charges.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple digital art takes 3-7 days, while complex websites can take 2-6 weeks. I'll provide a specific timeline during consultation.

Do you offer revisions?

Yes! Each package includes a specific number of revisions. I want to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final result.

What file formats do you provide?

I provide files in all standard formats (PNG, JPG, PDF, PSD, etc.) and can create custom formats based on your specific needs.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and can accommodate different time zones and communication preferences.

What's your payment process?

I typically require 50% upfront and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.
