Frequently Asked Questions

What is TMS therapy and how does it work?

TMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) is a non-invasive procedure that uses magnetic fields to stimulate nerve cells in the brain. It's FDA-approved for treating depression when other treatments haven't been effective.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major insurance plans, Medicare, and Medicaid. We also offer payment plans for patients without insurance coverage.

How long does a typical TMS treatment take?

Each TMS session typically lasts 20-40 minutes. The full course of treatment usually involves 5 sessions per week for 4-6 weeks.

What conditions do you treat?

We treat a wide range of mental health conditions including depression, anxiety, bipolar disorder, PTSD, addiction, and provide forensic psychiatric evaluations.
