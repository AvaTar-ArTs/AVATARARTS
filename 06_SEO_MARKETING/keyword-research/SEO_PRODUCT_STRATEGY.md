## üîó Backlink Signal Strategy

### What Are Backlink Signals?
| Element | Meaning |
|---------|---------|
| Referring Domain | The site linking to your product |
| Domain Authority (DA) | Score (1-100) predicting ranking power |
| Anchor Text | Clickable words used in the link |
| Link Type | Do-follow (passes authority) vs No-follow |
| Context | Where the link lives (blog, roundup, tweet) |
| Relevance | How aligned the linking site's niche is |

### Target Backlink Sources
| Source | DA Score | Type |
|--------|----------|------|
| geekcultureblog.com | 65 | Product roundup |
| humorbloggers.com | 58 | Viral gift mentions |
| redbubbletrends.substack | 71 | Weekly SEO drops |
| cyberstyle.today | 61 | Fashion blog |
| animewalls.io | 49 | Art showcase |

### How to Get Backlinks
1. **Pitch to gift guide bloggers** - Anime TikTokers, subreddit mods
2. **Create embeddable mockups** - Infographics and sharable images
3. **Publish "How I made this" posts** - Blog/Medium with cross-links
4. **Use HARO** - Help a Reporter Out for expert mentions
5. **Guest blog** - As Glitch Wizard/Quantum Artisan persona
