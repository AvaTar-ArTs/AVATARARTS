## üéØ AEO Keyword Strategy Framework

### What is Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)?

AEO optimizes content for AI-powered search engines that provide direct answers:
- **ChatGPT** (100M+ users)
- **Perplexity** (10M+ users)
- **Google AI Overviews** (billions of searches)
- **Microsoft Copilot** (growing rapidly)
- **Claude** (enterprise adoption)

### AEO vs Traditional SEO

| Factor | Traditional SEO | AEO Optimization |
|--------|----------------|------------------|
| **Query Type** | Short keywords | Conversational questions |
| **Content Format** | Web pages | Structured data, FAQs |
| **Answer Style** | Search results | Direct answers |
| **Ranking Factor** | Backlinks, domain authority | Content quality, accuracy |
| **Competition** | High (established sites) | Low (early stage) |

### AEO Keyword Characteristics

1. **Question-based**: "How to...", "What is...", "Why does...", "When should..."
2. **Conversational**: Natural language, long-tail
3. **Intent-focused**: Clear user intent (informational, transactional, navigational)
4. **Context-rich**: Include context and use cases
5. **Multi-format**: Optimized for text, code, lists, tables
