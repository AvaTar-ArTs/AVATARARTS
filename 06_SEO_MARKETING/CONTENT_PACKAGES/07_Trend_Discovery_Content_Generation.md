**Trend Discovery** is the process of identifying emerging topics, keywords, and content opportunities before they become mainstream. Advanced trend discovery systems use multi-platform intelligence to scan Twitter, Google, YouTube, Reddit, and other platforms to find topics with high growth potential.

### Trend Discovery Example

```python
from hot_trending_content_engine import HotTrendingContentEngine

# Initialize engine
engine = HotTrendingContentEngine()

# Discover hot trends
trends = engine.discover_hot_trends(
    niche="content creation, AI, automation",
    min_trend_score=75.0,
    max_results=10
)

# Process each trend
for trend in trends:
    print(f"Trend: {trend.keyword}")
    print(f"Score: {trend.score}/100")
    print(f"Growth: {trend.growth_rate}%")
    print(f"Platforms: {trend.platforms}")
    print("---")
    
    # Generate content
    package = engine.generate_trending_content(trend)
    
    print(f"Title: {package.title}")

    print(f"AEO Score: {package.aeo_score}/100")
    print("\n")
```
