üìã DistroKid Setup Guide (Recommended)

  • Create Account: Sign up at distrokid.com ($19.99/year for unlimited uploads)
  • Verify Identity: Complete artist verification and tax information (W-9 for US citizens)
  • Prepare Metadata: For each track, gather: Title, Artist Name, Genre, Release Date, Album Art (3000x3000px min)
  • Upload Tracks: Upload MP3 or WAV files (WAV recommended for best quality)
  • Set Release Date: Choose "Release as soon as possible" or schedule specific date
  • Select Platforms: Choose all available platforms (Spotify, Apple Music, Amazon, etc.)
  • Add Extras: Enable YouTube Content ID, store maximizer, and other add-ons
  • Review & Submit: Double-check everything and submit for distribution
  • Track Performance: Monitor streams and earnings in DistroKid dashboard
  • Get Paid: Set up direct deposit or PayPal for royalty payments
